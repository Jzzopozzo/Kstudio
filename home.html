We Dance From Our Hearts!!!

Kaustubh Joshi is a renowned choreographer, not just in Nashik, but also in Pune and Mumbai, Hyderabad, Goa and Jalgaon. He has been a participant in a Marathi Dance Show known as 'Khallas Dance Ekach Chance' where he gained a lot of fame for his versatility.

He has choreographed and also assisted mainstream choreographers for commercial movies and many reality shows. This makes him the most experienced person to learn dance from.

Kstudio has its own speciality while choreographing any song. Our students get a hands on experience of how it is like to be in front of camera and perform with not just hogh energy, but by adding each one's individual element into performance.
